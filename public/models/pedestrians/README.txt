Drop your pedestrian (walking NPC) .glb models here as:
  pedestrian1.glb, pedestrian2.glb, ... up to pedestrian20.glb

These spawn on the left & right sidewalks and walk along the street. One
file is enough to start; add more for variety (the game picks at random per
spawn).

Each pedestrian is auto-scaled to ~1.75m tall and grounded.

Facing: at source, the model should face +Z (toward the camera in three.js).
If your pedestrian faces backwards in game, re-export from Blender with
"Forward: -Z Forward" ticked on the GLB exporter.

Format: GLB (binary glTF 2.0).
